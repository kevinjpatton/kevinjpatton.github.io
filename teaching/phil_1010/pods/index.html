Pods (Under construction)

In my Meaning of Life course, I'm always looking for ways to help students engage with the complex and thought-provoking readings. This semester, I'm experimenting with using NotebookLM from Google to create podcasts that correspond to each reading.
The AI podcasts feature two hosts discussing the material in a conversational format, focusing on key aspects that I've highlighted. My hope is that this approach will help students follow the lines of reasoning in the texts and make the material more accessible.
What's more, I use these podcasts to help frame our class discussions. When the AI accurately captures the nuances of the material, it helps students connect with the concepts on a deeper level. Conversely, when the AI misses the mark, it provides a valuable opportunity to demonstrate the importance of critical thinking and not blindly relying on AI generated content.
I believe this is a productive way to use AI in education, as it allows me to provide students with an additional resource that complements their reading and enhances their understanding of the subject matter.
