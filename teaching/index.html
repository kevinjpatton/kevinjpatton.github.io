Teaching

As a graduate student at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln I have, however, moved back to Omaha for the time being, and am very thankful to be teaching at my alma mater which is the University of Nebraska - Omaha. For the Spring 2019 semester, I will be teaching four classes. Three of these classes will be a modified introduction to ethics class involving a survey of contemporary ethical theories, as well as contemporary ethical issues which are assessed using those aforementioned theories. The fourth class that I will be teaching is an upper-level Metaethics class. You can find the syllabus and the daily assignments under the Currently Teaching heading.
