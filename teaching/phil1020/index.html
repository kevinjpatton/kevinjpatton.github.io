Listen to episode 3 (Mirror Image). Caleb entered at one end of the rabbit hole and came out another. YouTube itself, and the social media platforms in general, are largely the same. If much of your day is consumed by some kind of online engagement (Twitter, the Tok, YouTube, etc.) then much of your day is being influenced by very sophisticated AI. Every person suggested to follow on Twitter is done so because you will (according to the AI) have a higher likelihood of engaging with that person's content.
