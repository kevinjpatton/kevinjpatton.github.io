Aug 27

Today we began discussing Mill's version of Consequentialism. For Wednesday, there is NO reading (woo) but please watch this Khan Academy video on the prisons' dilemma. As you watch this video, ask yourself how someone who listened to their conscience ALONE in determining what to do would do. Does this seem rational from an objective standpoint? We will discuss on Wednesday how considerations such as this lend support to Mill's claims at the end of chapter 3. Happy viewing!
