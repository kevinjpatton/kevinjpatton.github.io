Welcome

As a doctoral candidate in philosophy at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, I'm fascinated by questions of knowledge, reality, logic, language, science, the mind, and religion. My current research delves into the complexities of value in epistemology. While working on my dissertation, I have the privilege of teaching full-time as a visiting professor at my alma mater, the University of Nebraska - Omaha. If you're one of my students, you'll find course materials and updates under the Teaching tab.
