Welcome, students and philosophers alike! I am happy to report that I am a recently minted PhD, having just received my degree on August 2nd from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. My dissertation explores the orthodox approach to the value of knowledge and its associated problems. This orthodox approach is ultimately rejected, and a novel series of answers are offered to those long-standing problems. You can find out more about that by selecting "Dissertation" from the menu.

I'm also very fortunate to be a visiting professor at my alma mater, the University of Nebraska - Omaha. If you're a current student of mine, then you can access resources related to our class by selecting "Teaching" from the menu. If you wish to contact me, then please select "Contact" from the menu.
