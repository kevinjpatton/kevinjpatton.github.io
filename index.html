Welcome

I am a doctoral candidate in philosophy at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. My research interests include Metaphysics, Epistemology, Logic, the Philosophy of Language, Science, Mind, and Religion. My dissertation focuses on what are known as the value problem in epistemology. I am nearing completion of the dissertation and will (hopefully!) be defending either this spring or summer. I am currently a full-time visiting instructor for the University of Nebraska - Omaha (my alma mater).

My hobbies include powerlifting, anything computer related, gaming, and the remodeling and repairing of my home. I am heavily indebted to the opensource community as I wouldn't have been able to build this site in only a few days on GitHub if it wasn't for great explanations by Jonathan McGlone and W3Schools. Good teachers are invaluable!
