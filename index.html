Welcome, students and philosophers alike! I am happy to report that I am a recently minted PhD, having just received my degree on August 2nd from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. My dissertation explores the orthodox approach to the value of knowledge and its associated problems. You can find out more about that by selecting Dissertation from the menu above.

I'm also very fortunate to be an instructor at my alma mater, the University of Nebraska - Omaha. If you're a current student of mine, then you can access resources related to our class by selecting Teaching from the menu. I teach a broad range of courses, from introductory ethics and the meaning of life, to the philosophy of video games, ethical theory, and metaphysics. Additionally, I also teach a summer course that is offered to both undergraduate and graduate students at UNO.
