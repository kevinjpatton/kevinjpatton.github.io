The Philosophy of Video Games

This course explores video games as a unique art form, challenging definitions of art, narrative, and beauty while examining ethical complexities within games. Students will analyze the impact of video games on players and society, delving into deep philosophical dimensions through readings, discussions, and hands-on gameplay.
