Research

I'm fascinated by philosophical questions which revolve around knowledge, language, logic, science, the mind, reality, and religion. My current research delves into the intersection of epistemology and axiology, but I have also taught courses on value theory and metaethics, formal logic, and medical ethics. Additionally, I have been known to on occasion venture into the murky world of metaphysics (though stays there are often like Alice going to Wonderland). To view previous syllabi for my classes, please click the Teaching tab. As I (hopefully) publish in some of these various areas, I will also post those pieces below.
