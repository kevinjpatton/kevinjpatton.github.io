Topics of Interest

I'm fascinated by philosophical questions which revolve around knowledge, language, logic, science, the mind, reality, and religion. My current research delves into the intersection of epistemology and axiology, but I have taught courses on value theory, metaethics, formal logic, and medical ethics. I have also been known to venture - on occasion - into the murky world of metaphysics...though stays there are often like Alice going to Wonderland.
