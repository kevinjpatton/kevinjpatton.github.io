Research

I'm fascinated by philosophical questions which revolve around knowledge, language, logic, science, the mind, reality, and religion. My current research delves into the intersection of epistemology and axiology, but I regularly teach on issues related to axiology, metaethics, and formal logic. I have also been known to occasionally venture into the murky world of metaphysics (though stays there are often like Alice going to Wonderland). To view previous syllabi for those classes, please click the Teaching tab. As I (hopefully) publish in these various areas, I will post those pieces below.
