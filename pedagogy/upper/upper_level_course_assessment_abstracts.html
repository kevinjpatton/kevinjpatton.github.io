Upper-Level Section Summaries and Abstract Writing Assignment

This assignment aims to foster a deeper engagement with complex philosophical texts by requiring students to distill the essence of each section and synthesize the overarching argument in their own words. Every three weeks you will submit the following over two of the readings from that timeframe.

Assignment Components:

  1. Section Summaries: For each section of the assigned philosophical text, write a concise summary in your own words. Capture the main ideas, arguments, and evidence presented in each section. Aim for clarity and accuracy, avoiding direct quotes unless absolutely necessary.
  2. Overall Abstract: After summarizing each section, write a new abstract that encapsulates the entire paper's argument and key points. This abstract should be a stand-alone piece that accurately reflects the author's main thesis and supporting claims.

Purpose and Benefits:

  • Active Reading: Encourages close and critical reading of philosophical texts.
  • Comprehension: Ensures thorough understanding of each section's content.
  • Synthesis: Develops the ability to connect and integrate ideas across different sections.
  • Critical Thinking: Promotes analytical thinking and the ability to identify key arguments and evidence.
  • Writing Skills: Improves writing clarity, conciseness, and accuracy in summarizing complex ideas.
  • Research Skills: Prepares students for future research by practicing summarizing and synthesizing scholarly work.

Tips for Success:

  • Read Actively: Engage with the text by underlining key points, taking notes, and asking questions.
  • Focus on the Essentials: Identify the main ideas and arguments in each section, avoiding unnecessary details.
  • Use Your Own Words: Paraphrase the author's ideas to demonstrate your understanding and avoid plagiarism.
  • Connect the Dots: Show how each section contributes to the overall argument of the paper.
  • Revise and Refine: Edit your summaries and abstract for clarity, accuracy, and conciseness.

By completing this assignment, you will not only gain a deeper understanding of the assigned philosophical text but also develop valuable skills in reading, writing, and critical thinking that will serve you well throughout your academic and professional career.
